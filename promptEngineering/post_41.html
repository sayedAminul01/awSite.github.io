How to Use DALL·E 3 Inside ChatGPT: Full Guide with Examples

Creating images with AI has become easier than ever. One of the latest breakthroughs is the integration of DALL·E 3 directly into ChatGPT, allowing users to generate stunning visuals from simple text prompts. Whether you’re a designer, marketer, content creator, or just exploring AI tools, this guide will walk you through using DALL·E 3 effectively.

Step-by-Step: Using DALL·E 3 in ChatGPT

When you activate GPT-4 in ChatGPT and enable image generation features, you’ll have direct access to the power of DALL·E 3. Instead of needing a separate tool or website, you can type your prompts right into ChatGPT and receive images in seconds.

Prompt Example 1: Logo Design

Let’s say you need a logo for an organic tea brand named “LeafBrew.” You could ask ChatGPT: "Generate a minimalist logo for an eco-friendly tea brand called LeafBrew, with green leaf elements and a calming style."

DALL·E 3 will interpret the style and mood from your prompt and create visually unique logos. You can then view each result, click to see the prompt it used, and tweak the prompt to refine your design.

Prompt Example 2: Character Illustration

Imagine you’re writing a children’s book and want a cute character. You could type: "Create a playful panda playing guitar under the moonlight, in watercolor style."

The result? A beautifully illustrated image that brings your scene to life, perfect for storytelling or publishing use.

New Feature: Text in Images

What makes DALL·E 3 even more impressive is its ability to accurately render text within images. Unlike older AI image tools, this feature makes it perfect for generating posters, memes, comics, product labels, and more.

Example: Ask it to create a billboard with the words “Drive Safe, Live Well” and it will place that text clearly on the image. You can even request different fonts, layouts, or languages.

Combining Text & Image: Visual Proposals

Need a proposal or presentation slide with matching imagery? Try: "Write a short paragraph about an innovative eco-gadget and generate an image that visually explains its purpose."

This merges content writing and design into one effortless process—ideal for businesses and educators alike.

Tips to Get Better Results with Prompts

  • Use descriptive adjectives (e.g., serene, metallic, vintage)
  • Define the art style (e.g., oil painting, 3D render, anime)
  • Specify background details (e.g., cloudy sky, wooden floor)
  • Try different variations and tweak based on output

Advanced Use: Midjourney-Style Prompts

You can input prompts just like you would in tools like Midjourney. For example: "A futuristic cityscape viewed from above at night, cinematic style, with glowing lights and light fog."

DALL·E 3 understands complex artistic commands, making it versatile for creative professionals.

SEO Tip: Naming and Saving Your Images

Always rename your generated images with meaningful filenames and ALT tags (e.g., "eco-friendly-logo-leafbrew.png"). This improves image SEO for your blog or website.

Additional Creative Ideas:

  • Design merchandise mockups (t-shirts, mugs)
  • Visualize interior designs
  • Create social media content like quotes or banners
  • Generate educational visuals and infographics

By integrating DALL·E 3 with ChatGPT, your creative workflow becomes smoother, smarter, and more efficient.
