Enhance ChatGPT Prompts with Modifiers for Better Results

One of the easiest ways to improve the quality of answers you receive from ChatGPT is by using *prompt modifiers*. These are simple words or phrases that clarify what you want and how you want it, giving you more precise, tailored responses.

Why Modifiers Matter

Let’s say you ask: "Suggest places to visit in a city". That’s vague. But if you say: "Suggest five fun places to visit for a weekend trip in a coastal city", now you’ve added modifiers like number (five), type (fun), duration (weekend), and location type (coastal). These little tweaks help the AI deliver exactly what you’re looking for.

Types of Useful Modifiers

  • Qualifiers: Words like “some,” “many,” or “few” to define scope. Example: “Give me several productivity tools.”
  • Adjectives: Words describing nouns, like “quick,” “simple,” “effective.” E.g., “Write a simple budget plan.”
  • Adverbs: Words modifying actions or adjectives, like “quickly,” “efficiently.” E.g., “Explain it clearly and briefly.”
  • Negatives: Words like “don’t,” “not,” “never.” Useful to exclude results. E.g., “List healthy snacks not containing nuts.”
  • Numbers: “Two,” “few,” “ten.” These define list size or quantity. E.g., “Suggest three side hustles.”
  • Time Words: “Today,” “soon,” “during summer.” Help define temporal context. E.g., “Things to prepare before winter.”
  • Place Words: “Nearby,” “in remote areas,” “in urban settings.” These add location relevance. E.g., “Tips for working remotely from rural areas.”
  • Degree Words: “Completely,” “slightly,” “very.” Adjust intensity. E.g., “Make it very persuasive.”

Applying Modifiers in Prompts

Imagine you're writing a blog post using AI. You can use modifiers to guide tone, structure, and style:

  • "Write a witty introduction for a post about freelancing."
  • "Summarize the key points briefly in bullet points."
  • "Give me five easy home workouts without equipment."

Extra Tips for Better Prompts

  • Be specific: Instead of “Explain climate change,” try “Explain climate change for middle school students in three short paragraphs.”
  • Iterate: Don’t settle for the first response. Tweak your modifiers to adjust the results.
  • Combine techniques: Use modifiers with role-setting like “Act as a digital marketing coach and explain SEO in a friendly tone.”

Conclusion

Modifiers are powerful tools in prompt engineering. They help ChatGPT understand exactly what you want. Whether it’s tone, scope, depth, or style—your words guide the results. Practice using different types of modifiers, and you’ll notice a big improvement in the usefulness of AI responses.
