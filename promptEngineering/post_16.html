1. Client and Customer Support Prompt Formula

This formula is ideal for creating professional, empathetic responses to customer queries or complaints. Here's the basic template:

I want you to act as a customer support assistant who is [describe personality trait]. How would you respond to a customer who is facing [specific issue] as a representative of our [type of business]?

Example: I want you to act as a customer support assistant who is calm and detail-oriented. How would you respond to a user who is having trouble submitting a form on our educational platform?

2. Creating Analogies to Understand Concepts

Understanding complex topics is easier with analogies. Use this prompt to simplify any subject:

I'm trying to better understand the concept of [topic]. Please help by creating a simple, relatable analogy.

Example: I'm trying to understand how data encryption works. Please provide an analogy that helps me visualize it clearly.

This technique is incredibly helpful in teaching, learning, or simplifying difficult topics for your audience.
