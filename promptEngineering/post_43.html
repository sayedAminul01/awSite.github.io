Easy Guide to Creating and Editing Images with DALL·E 3 Inside ChatGPT

Have you heard? Now you can create amazing, high-quality images directly inside ChatGPT using DALL·E 3 — no extra apps needed.

Simply type a description of what you want to see, like "a peaceful lake at sunset with colorful trees," and within moments, the AI generates a beautiful image matching your words.

One of the coolest features is Inpainting. Imagine you generated a picture of a city skyline but want to change the sky to a dramatic sunset or add a hot air balloon floating in the air. You don’t need to start over — just select the area you want to change, describe what you want added or adjusted, and the AI updates the image for you.

Here’s a smart tip: after editing an image with Inpainting, DALL·E 3 creates a new text prompt describing the updated picture. You can copy this prompt and use it to generate more images with similar styles or edits, which helps keep your visuals consistent if you are working on a series.

This integration of DALL·E 3 into ChatGPT streamlines your creative workflow. Whether you’re a graphic designer, storyteller, or hobbyist exploring AI art, these tools make image creation intuitive and flexible.

Try creating a fantasy forest scene or a futuristic cityscape, then edit details like lighting, objects, or colors with ease — all without leaving ChatGPT.

Start experimenting today, and let your imagination shape stunning visuals with the power of AI right at your fingertips.
