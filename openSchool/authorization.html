About BOSSE

The Board of Open Schooling & Skill Education (BOSSE), established under Act No. 14 of 2020 by the Government of Sikkim, is an innovative educational board that offers flexible and skill-oriented learning solutions. BOSSE is recognized for its commitment to making education accessible and meaningful, especially for students seeking alternative learning pathways.
