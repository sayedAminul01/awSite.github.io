Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ's)

What is BOSSE?
The Board of Open Schooling and Skill Education (BOSSE) is an open schooling board recognized by the Government of Sikkim, established under Act No. 14 of 2020. It aims to meet the diverse academic needs of students up to the pre-degree level, offering secondary, senior secondary, skill, and vocational education. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}
Is BOSSE a recognized board?
Yes, BOSSE is recognized by the Government of Sikkim and is listed by the Ministry of Education, Government of India, in the approved list of school boards. :contentReference[oaicite:1]{index=1}
What courses does BOSSE offer?
BOSSE offers courses at three levels: Secondary (equivalent to Class X), Senior Secondary (equivalent to Class XII), and Skill and Vocational Education. :contentReference[oaicite:2]{index=2}
Who is eligible to enroll in BOSSE programs?
Individuals who have completed 15 years of age can apply for admission to the Secondary Programme, even if they have not undergone any formal education earlier. A person who has passed Class X can apply for admission to the Senior Secondary Programme. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}
What mediums of study are available in BOSSE?
BOSSE offers study materials and conducts examinations in multiple mediums to cater to the diverse linguistic needs of students. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}
How does BOSSE support students during their studies?
BOSSE provides continuous 24/7 support throughout the academic journey, offering access to dedicated student advisors, technical assistance, and a wealth of online resources to enrich the learning experience. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}
What are the examination schedules in BOSSE?
BOSSE conducts examinations twice a year, typically in April–May and October–November. Results are usually announced six weeks after the last date of examinations. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}
