About Us

Welcome to AlienWeb, your trusted partner in the digital world. At AlienWeb, we are more than just a service provider; we are a community-driven business committed to empowering individuals and businesses through technology. Founded by Sayed Aminul, a passionate entrepreneur with a deep-rooted desire to uplift his community, AlienWeb offers a range of services tailored to meet the needs of today's fast-paced digital landscape.

Our Story

AlienWeb began its journey in 2019 in the heart of Chauliabari, Bajali, Assam, with a simple yet powerful mission: to bring technology and education to the rural areas. Starting with a small Cyber Cafe, we quickly expanded our services to include Computer Vocational Training through our institute, Alien Institute of Computer. Over the years, we've helped countless students and professionals gain the skills they need to succeed in the digital age.

In 2023, we added a new dimension to our offerings with the launch of our Open Schooling and Skills Education service. This initiative allows dropouts, housewives, workers, and anyone who wishes to complete their Class X and XII education in Arts, Science, or Commerce streams, all from the comfort of their local area. Our commitment to education doesn't stop there—we also provide free training to underprivileged students because we believe that education is the key to a better future.

Our Services

At AlienWeb, we offer a comprehensive suite of services designed to help you succeed online:

  • Computer Vocational Courses: We offer a variety of courses that equip students with practical, job-ready skills in fields like web development, graphic design, and digital marketing.
  • Open Schooling for Class IX to XII: Our flexible open schooling program allows students to complete their education in Arts, Science, or Commerce, at their own pace.
  • Freelancing Services: From virtual assistance to social media management, our team is ready to help you manage your business more efficiently, so you can focus on what you do best.
